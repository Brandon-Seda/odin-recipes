Mangu Recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 green plantains
  • 1 quart water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced white onion
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
  • 1 cup sliced Anaheim peppers

Steps

  1. Place the plantains and water in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, and cook 20 minutes, until plantains are tender but slightly firm.
  2. Drain, reserving 1 cup of the liquid. Cool plantains, and peel.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and saute the onion until tender.
  4. In a bowl, mash the plantains with the reserved liquid and salt. Transfer to a food processor, mix in the peppers, and puree.
  5. Serve the pureed plantain mixture topped with the onions.
